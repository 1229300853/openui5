Usage of the OpenUI5 name and logo

Feel free to spread the word about OpenUI5 and use our logo.
Be sure to follow these few guidelines when doing so:

Name

It is OpenUI5 - capital O, capital UI, no spaces between Open and UI5

Logo

Please download our logo set or save the following logos individually:

OpenUI5 Logo only

OpenUI5 Logo with text below

OpenUI5 Logo with text on the right side

✘ Don't:

  • Change the colors, font or text style
  • Add effects (like shadow, glow)
  • Mix it with other content

Colors

The main brand color is the OpenUI5 Blue:
#009DE0

Appearance

✔ Do:

  • Use our logo for all your needs (presentations, products, websites, merchandise, â€¦)
  • Show a clear differentiation between your initiative/service/product and the OpenUI5 brand
  • Clearly state that you are a partner or community project and link to our official website
Example: You are allowed to initiate a group called "Munich OpenUI5 User Group" and register and operate the domain "openui5-users-MUC.org".

✘ Don't:

  • Pretend to be officialy affiliated with OpenUI5 unless authorized by the OpenUI5 team (e.g. "The Official OpenUI5 Forum")
  • Register and operate official looking accounts or websites (e.g. registering the "OpenUI5" account in a social network)
  • Publish and replicate content or source code from our official website (creating redundancies), link to us instead
