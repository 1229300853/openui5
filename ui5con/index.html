learn.

At UI5con rookies, experienced and expert developers are gathering to learn more about the framework and its features from each other. The UI5 framework enables you to build enterprise-ready web applications, responsive to all devices, running on almost any browser of your choice. It’s based on JavaScript and eases your development with a client-side HTML5 rendering library including a rich set of controls while supporting data binding to different models.

explore.

UI5con is the one event for UI5 enthusiasts to meet in person and find out what’s happening in the UI5 world. There’s a UI5 community with more than 3000 developers reaching from novices to professionals scattered all over the world, working on their own UI5 related projects. Discussions about new features and best practices float through the world wide web, so it’s high time to face each other and explore the many facets of UI5.

connect.

The event itself has been molded by the UI5 community, so everyone is warmly welcome to join this huge family! Meet fellow UI5 developers who love what you love, who see the world through your eyes and who struggle with the same everyday problems the way you do. UI5con is meant to connect all the geeks out there - have a common platform to get to know each other, discuss innovations and find solutions.
